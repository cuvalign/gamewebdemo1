About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced roguelike survival game where you must battle endless waves of monsters. Every run is a unique experience with random enemy spawns, powerful items, and new challenges to overcome.

Inspired by games like Vampire Survivors, this game combines action with strategic character building. As the difficulty increases, how long can you survive? Test your limits and become the ultimate survivor!

Features

Explore the rich features and systems of Monster Survivors

Diverse Characters

Choose from different characters, each with unique starting weapons and special abilities.

Weapon Evolution

Combine weapons and passive items to create powerful evolved weapons with special effects.

Upgrade System

Defeat enemies to gain experience, level up, and choose new weapons or ability upgrades.

Boss Battles

Face giant boss monsters at special intervals and push your limits.

Treasure Chests

Find and open chests to get random items, coins, or special power-ups.

Daily Challenges

Complete daily challenges to earn rewards and climb the global leaderboard.

Controls

Keyboard Controls

WASD Move character
Arrow Keys Move character (alternative)
Space Pause game
E/Enter Select/Confirm
ESC Back/Menu

Mobile Controls

Virtual Joystick

A virtual joystick appears on the left side of the screen for character movement.

Touch Buttons

Menu and action buttons on the right side of the screen.

Swipe Gestures

Swipe to navigate menus and selection screens.
